A bubble Cursor, based on the 2005 paper "The bubble cursor: enhancing target acquisition by dynamic resizing of the cursor's activation area" by Grossman and Balakrishnan.

If HTML5/Javascript runs slowly in your browser (for example, Firefox 3.X), the application will render slowly. Try a fully compliant browser, such as Firefox 4, Chrome, or Safari.
